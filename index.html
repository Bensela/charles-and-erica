Ev'ry day we'll serve our God above. As he shows the way, unfailing love we'll display. As we have vowed, so may it be. Seasons of joy, may we come to see. Oh, may we honor Jehovah, and may you always be my love.

Guidance

Ceremony Notes

Mobile Phone Use

Please set your device appropriately so as not to disturb the program.

Washrooms

Washrooms are available for all. Please keep the place clean after use.

Expression of Joy

This is a happy occasion, but excessive expression of joy such as shouting «Ayefroo Dondoo» and other slogans are not encouraged.

Practices to Avoid

Throwing confetti or the like (rice, perfume etc.) over the couple is to be avoided.

Photographs

If you like to take personal pictures, you can do so from your seat or in a manner that will not cause distraction. We appeal to you not to post pictures of the couple or upload them on the internet or social media (Facebook, Twitter, Youtube, WhatsApp). We will follow the order on the Program in taking photographs.

Gifts

Please if you have any gift for the couple, you can give it to their representative seated at the gift table.
